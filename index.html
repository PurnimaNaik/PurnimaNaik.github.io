About

A strategic thinker who strives to come up with simple solutions to complex problems and deliver them through alluring applications.

I specialize in creating native/hybrid mobile and web applications for a variety of platforms using React Native/JS, Objective C, Java, Kotlin and JavaScript.
Some highlights of my professional work experience have been developing a production ready IOT app from scratch, under 5 months, for a wireless EV charging company and delivering 5 different applications under 6 months for the R&D department of a retail business chain.

Aside from traditional software development roles, I have worked as a Digital Marketing SEO Analyst for over a year. While in the role, I spent most of my time identifying business relevant keywords, creating link silos, modifying website meta data, redesigning website architecture and incorporating keyword rich, unique content.

Organizing and leading a project is a strong asset of mine. I particularly take interest in requirement gathering, defining deliverables and establishing milestones with a well thought out timeline.

I also enjoy the designing process of app making and have created the icons, logos, images and other graphic assets for my personal projects using Adobe XD.
